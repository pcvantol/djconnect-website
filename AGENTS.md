## Role

This repo owns the public product story, onboarding, website UX, community/personal positioning and documentation entry points.

## Rules

- Website copy should explain DJConnect as an AI music companion, not as a Spotify/Home Assistant feature list.
- Community: “Your AI DJ understands music.”
- Personal: “Your AI DJ understands you.”
- Do not market future Cloud features as current capabilities.
- Keep cloud DJ personas, premium voices, hosted AI and sync as future-facing unless implemented.
- Product language must align with the Constitution and Product Vision.
- Prefer experience-led messaging: listen, understand, feel, continue, share the room.
