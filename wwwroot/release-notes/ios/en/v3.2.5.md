# DJConnect App 3.2.5

- iPhone and Apple Watch pairing now each show only their own QR action, with
  manual entry as the fallback.
- Apple Watch pairing is clearer: iPhone and Watch now explicitly show that the
  Apple Watch is paired after completion.
- The Music DNA opt-in sheet now matches the calmer Share Feedback sheet style.
- Track Insight shows a lock icon next to the privacy footer, and log line
  numbers are muted light gray.
